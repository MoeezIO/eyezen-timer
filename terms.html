Our Mission

At EyeZen, we believe that digital productivity shouldn't come at the cost of your health. With millions of people working remotely, digital eye strain has become a global health concern.

The Science

The 20-20-20 rule is a scientifically-backed method recommended by optometrists. By looking at something 20 feet away for 20 seconds every 20 minutes, you allow your eye muscles to relax and reset. This reduces headaches, blurred vision, and dry eyes.

Why We Built This

We wanted to create a tool that was minimalist, free, and beautiful. No bulky app installs, no sign-ups—just a simple web tool that helps you stay healthy while you work. EyeZen combines the 20-20-20 rule with hydration tracking and breathing exercises to provide a full wellness experience for the modern professional.
