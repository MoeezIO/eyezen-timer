About EyeZen

EyeZen is a wellness tool designed to help you follow the 20-20-20 rule: Every 20 minutes, focus on something 20 feet away for 20 seconds.

This simple habit significantly reduces digital eye strain and Computer Vision Syndrome (CVS) caused by long hours at the screen.

Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously. EyeZen does not collect, store, or share any of your personal data.

Your statistics (breaks, water intake, screen time) are stored strictly in your browser's local cache (LocalStorage). No data ever leaves your device.

Support EyeZen

EyeZen is free to use for everyone. You can support the project by considering the recommended wellness products through the affiliate links on our page.

Your support through these small actions helps us maintain the website and keep it ad-free!
